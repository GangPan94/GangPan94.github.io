## The Story of Malady

<img src="/images/cats/malady.jpg" alt="Malady" style="float:right; max-width:300px; margin:0 0 12px 20px; border-radius:8px;" />

Malady was once someone else's cat before she was abandoned. She's friendly, affectionate, playful, and always curious. When she was surrendered to the Cleveland APL, she was still a kitten but already a mom of four! With the overpopulation of cats in the US, it's important to help them find homes and control their numbers.

When I visited Cleveland, I was on the lookout for a cat that resembled Emma in both appearance and personality. And then I found Malady! She's just like Emma after a haircut. Malady mostly speaks in her second tone.
