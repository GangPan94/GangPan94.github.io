## The Story of Benji

I stopped by the Humane Society of Summit County on my way back from a haircut, with absolutely no intention of adopting another cat. I was just there to check out the giant facility.

I made that very clear to one of the volunteers, telling her I already had five cats at home. She said she has ten, and that five really isn’t that many.

I told her again that I was not looking for a cat. She smiled and said, “Sometimes, it’s not that you’re looking for a cat; it’s the cat looking for you.”

Right then, Benji seemed very determined to go home with me. If you’d like to see the moment we first met, watch the video below. And for the record, I do not regret taking him home one bit.
