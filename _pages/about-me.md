## "Gang of One" and Why "Ernest"

My Chinese name is **刚** (gāng), meaning "tough" or "strong." However, "Gang" in English refers to a group, which is why people at drive-throughs smile when they hear my name — there's no such thing as a gang of one! To avoid confusion, I chose the name "Ernest." I like it because of Ernest Hemingway, who wrote about a man who refused to be defeated. The old man's fight with a giant marlin and the sharks beautifully captures the meaning of **刚**.
