## The Story of Atusa and Clayton

<img src="/images/cats/atusa-clayton.png" alt="Atusa and Clayton" style="float:right; max-width:300px; margin:0 0 12px 20px; border-radius:8px;" />

Meet Atusa and Clayton, a bonded pair who came home together because they belong together. They are affectionate, adventurous, and full of personality, with a real engineer’s talent for testing limits and finding clever solutions.

They also have a knack for challenging my catio security. Clayton is winning so far, since he has found a weak spot he can squeeze through. Atusa, who is bigger, has not been able to fit through it. Both are still learning how to adventure safely outdoors and both need more leash training. They have already gotten stuck in a tree before, which is a good reminder that curiosity and caution do not always arrive at the same time.
