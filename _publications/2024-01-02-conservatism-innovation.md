Solo-authored.
- Conservatism or pay for performance alone cannot effectively induce innovation.
- Combining conservatism and high bonuses is associated with better innovation.
- My results support Laux and Ray (2020)'s view that more conservatism increases the informativeness of good signals, allowing higher bonuses for high outcomes.
