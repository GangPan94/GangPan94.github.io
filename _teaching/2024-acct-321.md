  Focuses on the conceptual framework of Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and the detailed accounting for assets and liabilities on the balance sheet. This course guides students through the accounting cycle, financial statement presentation, and the time value of money, with a specific focus on measuring and reporting key accounts such as cash, receivables, inventory, long-lived assets, current liabilities, and bonds. Students develop core analytical skills and learn how to use Generative AI tools to analyze standard financial data.
