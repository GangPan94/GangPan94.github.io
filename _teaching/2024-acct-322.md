  Examines complex corporate transactions and standard-setting dynamics. Building on the balance sheet fundamentals from ACCT 321, this course covers advanced topics such as revenue recognition, lease financing, investment portfolios, employee pensions, and share-based compensation. Particular emphasis is placed on two high-stakes areas: accounting for corporate income taxes and constructing the statement of cash flows. Students gain practical experience using Generative AI for corporate disclosure analysis and policy research.
